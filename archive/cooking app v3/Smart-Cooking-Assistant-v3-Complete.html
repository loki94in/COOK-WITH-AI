Chef's Tips (from video)

Always soak basmati rice for at least 30 minutes — it makes the grains longer and fluffier.
Don't skip the dum (slow cooking) — it's what gives biryani its signature smoky flavour.
The rice should be 70% cooked before layering — it finishes cooking in the dum.
